BESTMAP Water Quality - Nutrient Delivery Ratio model - Nitrogen and Phosphorus

The objective of this model is to map nutrient sources, from watershed to stream. It allows nutrient retention by natural vegetation in relation to surface water quality to be investigated (Sharp et al., 2018). As a result, the outputs of this model are the following layers:

  • Water Quality - Nutrient Delivery Ratio Nitrogen: nitrogen surface export in kg/year/km.
  • Water Quality - Nutrient Delivery Ratio Phosphorus: phosphorus surface export kg/year/km.

Each layer consists of 3 different pieces of information which the user can select from the three available dropdown lists:

  • Scenario: either with AES or no AES, indicating the scenario with current Agri-Environment Schemes (AES) adoption, or the one without AES;
  • Borselli_k: it indicates the value of the parameter Borselli K (either 1, 2 or 4);
  • Flow accumulation: it indicates the values of the parameter threshold flow accumulation (either 100, 1000 or 10000).
